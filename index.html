The UNHCR Refugee database contains data from the year 2000 to 2012; recently, data is available until mid 2013. The available data gives information about the refugees general composition by location of residence or origin, their status (refugees, asylum seekers, internally displaced persons) and their evolution over time. The data is discretionary in a scientific context (DATA_SOURCES_AND_PROVIDERS).

How to use this map?

1) To select a country please click on the one you prefer to get information about or use the drop down list and choose the country you like

2) By sliding over the countries you will get the country name as information

3) In the first diagram in the manual side bar, you get more information about refugees over time of the selected country

4) Use the mousewheel to zoom or click on the zoom button in the manual side bar

5) You can change the migration between - refugees who have left their origin - and the ones who have fled into the country you selected

6) To filter the mass of information and connections between two countries, please use the drop down list and choose the type of migration

7) The time slider shows changes of the number of refugees based on the selected country

8) If you like to get more information about the changes of migration between two time steps click twice on the time slider and choose what you like to compare.
